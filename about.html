About Furry Friends

Furry Friends was established in 2014 to provide the furry, and not so furry, family members with a wide variety of services while there owners may be on vacation, at work, and need someone to look after their furry ones. We tailor these services to best fit the owners and pet(s) schedules. These incldue visiting the home and feeding and walking pets during scheduled times. In addition, we have sitters that allow the pets to stay overnight, or, as needed, to provide companionship and comfort for the pet. We also provide additional services, such as bathing and grooming.

Our sitters recieve training needed for caring for your pets regardles of situation. Our training allows the sitter to handle emergency situations in a calm and confident manner. Knowledge concerning basic pet first aid, such as handling burns, choking or other injuries is a must for pet sitters.
